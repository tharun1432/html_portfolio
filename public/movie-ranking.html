The Best Movies According to Tharun

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Ee Nagaraniki Emaindi

This is my favourite film. The story is based on four childhood friends who go on a trip to Goa.

KGF Chapter 1

A gangster named Rocky goes undercover as a slave to assassinate the owner of a notorious gold mine known as the K.G.F.

Oh My Dog

Young Arjun rescues a blind Siberian Husky puppy and decides to train him for a competition with his father and grandfather.
